### Minor Violations:
- **First offense:** Warning + logged
- **Second offense:** Stern warning + 24-hour restriction
- **Third offense:** Week-long restriction

### Major Violations:
- **First offense:** Permanent restriction on specific feature
- **Second offense:** Permanent AI access removal

### Critical Violations (Illegal Activity):
- **Immediate action:** Complete AI access termination
- **Logging:** Incident logged to secure, tamper-proof storage

  - Terrorism → FBI/DHS
  - Other crimes → Appropriate authorities
